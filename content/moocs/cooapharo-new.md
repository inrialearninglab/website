## Objectif pédagogique / Learning Objective
**You will get to know and understand the key mechanisms and the essence of Object Oriented Design and discover Test-Driven Design (TDD).**

At the end of this course, you will be able to:

- explain elementary concepts around self, super, and the power of dispatching to dedicated objects
- test applications and how to take advantage of test driven design.
- design abstractions that are extensible using hooks.
- make the difference between data, objects and classes, based on basic principles.
- apply important design patterns such as composite and state.
- compare usage versus inheritance a way to understand the forces in presence in a design.
- apply double dispatch mechanism and the visitor design pattern which is based on it.
- select patterns to create and initialize objects.
- use different techniques and design patterns to share objects.
- explain the law of demeter and different techniques to achieve Inversion of control.
- explain typing and its relation with object-oriented design.

Having a first knowledge on object-oriented programming is better to fully understand the advanced topics of this MOOC.
