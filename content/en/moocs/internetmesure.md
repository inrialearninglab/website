## Course description
 This course is a practical introduction to Internet measurements (metrology), based on real experiments on the PlanetLab Europe platform. It covers various concepts such as network topology and routing, losses, latency, geolocation, bandwidth and traffic measurements. The videos in this MOOC are also subtitled in French.
 
## Who is this course for?
People with the level of understanding of internet technology that comes from an advanced undergraduate course or a first Masters course in networking, or equivalent professional experience.
 
## Course outline
- Module 1: **Introduction**
- Module 2: **Topology and routes**
- Module 3: **Connectivity, losses, latency, and geolocation**
- Module 4: **Bandwidth**
- Module 5: **Traffic Measurements**
